Create/Edit new notification
1. Notification type
    a. Price
    b. GL
    b. GL%
    c. Total GL
    d. Total GL%

2. Notification options:
    a. Only once
    b. Everytime condition reaches

3. Condition:
    a. More than
    b. Less than
    c. Equal to

4. Expiration date

5. Coin

JSON format:

{
    "id": "type_coin_timestamp",
    "type": "type",
    "option": 'once' || 'everytime',
    "expiry": 'date' (note, we will take the end of day for the provided date)
}
